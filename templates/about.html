About Us

The Face of Hope is a global initiative leveraging AI-powered face recognition technology to reunite missing persons with their families. We work alongside law enforcement, NGOs, and volunteers to create a fast, secure, and reliable system for locating lost individuals. Our platform allows for anonymous submissions, real-time alerts, and a growing network of thousands of registered profiles, ensuring that no one remains lost.

Our Mission

We believe in using technology for good. Our mission is to bring hope to families by reuniting them with their loved ones. Join us in making a difference.
